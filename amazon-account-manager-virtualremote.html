Amazon Account Manager - Virtual/Remote

8/24/2023

 
Job Description:
Join Amazon as a Virtual/Remote Account Manager. In this role, you'll manage key client relationships, drive growth, and ensure client satisfaction. Leverage your skills from anywhere to contribute to Amazon's success. This position offers flexibility and the opportunity to work remotely while collaborating with a global team. As an Account Manager, you'll play a vital role in Amazon's business ecosystem, delivering exceptional solutions and services to clients. Elevate your career with Amazon, virtually.

When you join the team, not only will you become an Amazon expert, but you will play a key role in the success of each of our clients. Our Account Managers have been able to turn small accounts into seven and eight-figure brands on Amazon through strategic selling.

You will also get to use our own proprietary myHorizons analytics tool that is designed to make brand management easier and more effective.

Salary: $60,000 - $75,000 USD + bonus
Location: US-based - Fully Remote (Working hours are 8:00am - 6:00pm EST Mon-Fri)

As an Account Manager you will be responsible for managing a portfolio of emerging retailers and developing partnerships in a highly collaborative team environment.

Account Managers wear several hats when managing brands, but your core responsibilities will be creation and implementation of brand growth strategy, analyzing trends and reports, Product Detail Page optimizations, Pay Per Click Advertising, Display Advertising and more.

How You Will Make A Difference:
Account Managers play an active part in growing emerging brands on Amazon. By developing a working knowledge of our partners and their business strategies, Account Managers build strong client relationships and become trusted partners for eCommerce strategy.

Your Responsibilities:
Owning client relationships and having strong communication with clients and other Envision Horizons team members
Optimizing Advertising on Amazon such as Amazon Sponsored Product Ads, Sponsored Brand Ads, Display Ads
Prioritizing Tasks and staying organized while balancing several brands and making sure that everything is operating smoothly
Coordinating with Account Associates and Virtual Assistants to ensure task completion

Culture
Who are we?

Envision Horizons is a leading Amazon Agency recently nominated for Amazon Advertising's 2022 Brand Building Award and featured in major business publications such as Forbes and Business Insider. Founded in 2017, Envision Horizons is a turnkey solution for brands looking to make selling on Amazon less of a headache. Our mission is to help brands of all sizes optimize their product pages, navigate common troubleshooting issues, and strategically utilize Amazon Advertising to successfully scale brands on Amazon.

Our Core Values:
  • Be Inquisitive - be naturally curious, love working with data, and determined to find an answer.
  • Be A Leader - think like a business owner and be confident in your knowledge and expertise.
  • Get It Done - Get it done and get it done right, if you don’t know how to get it done, be resourceful and independent in your work.
  • Have Humility - Admit when you’re wrong and take it as a learning opportunity. Approach all conflict with active listening and compassion
